Website Evaluations

Twitch

A interactive livestreaming service for content spanning gaming, entertainment, sports, music, and more

  • Contrast: The contrast is very appealing and easy on eyes. The font will stand out as it will contrast itself from the background.
  • Repitition: A theme of colors such as black, white, and purple with the same font of varying sizes.
  • Alignment: The text is aligned to the left.
  • Proximity: Every elements that are grouped together have a uniform space to separate from other elements

Imgur

A online image sharing and image hosting service

  • Contrast: the contrast isn't as professional as the background lacks contrast; font is hard to read at times.
  • Repitition The images are the only thing that are in repition.
  • Alignment: The images are aligned in the center but depending on image sizes, it makes it not organized
  • Proximity: Cluttered with images, and the header glides down as you scroll giving it a awkward space between other elements used.
